USA Heat Map

Continent Population Trends

This simple line graph depicts the population over time in each continent. This is important as it was one of our first visuals and was a stepping stone in helpings us understand the relationships in the data.









Interactive Continents Scatter Plot

Here is a fun interactive scatter plot that allows you to change two inputs: the dependent variable and the relation (the data point size). You can change the inputs and see the different trends displayed on the scatter plot. BONUS! You can use the choropleth map below to see how female participation in the labor force affects the scatter plots!





Choropleth Map

Here is a visual map that shows the female labor participation rate of all the countries. Use the dropdown to see the different rates per year form 1990s to 2021. You can use this and view it side by side with the scatter plot above and see how more females working affect different population factors.
